Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

At KindFund Foundation, we take the utmost care to process donations as per the instructions given by our donors, online and offline. However, we understand that errors may occur. This policy outlines the circumstances under which a refund may be requested.

1. General Policy

As a non-profit organization, KindFund utilizes donations immediately for our charitable programs (food distribution, education fees, etc.). Therefore, donations once made are generally non-refundable.

2. Valid Grounds for Refund

We will consider a refund request only under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Error: If the same amount was debited twice due to a technical glitch during the payment process.
  • Unauthorized Transaction: If the donor can prove that the transaction was made without their consent (e.g., stolen card).
  • Human Error: If a donor mistakenly enters a significantly incorrect amount (e.g., ₹10,000 instead of ₹1,000).

3. Request Process

To request a refund, you must contact us within 7 days of the transaction date. Please send an email to support@kindfund.org with the following details:

1. Date of Donation
2. Donation Amount
3. Transaction ID / Reference ID
4. Reason for Refund
5. Proof of deduction (Screenshot/Bank Statement)

4. Processing Timeline

Once a request is received, our finance team will verify the claim within 3 business days. If approved, the refund will be initiated to the original payment method (Credit Card/Bank Account/UPI). The amount may take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account depending on your bank's policies.

5. Tax Receipt Implications

Important Note regarding 80G:

If a refund is issued, the tax exemption certificate (Form 10BE) issued for that specific donation will be marked as void/cancelled. You cannot claim tax benefits for refunded donations.
