Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

At KindFund Foundation, we take the utmost care to process donations. However, we understand that errors may occur. This policy outlines the circumstances under which a refund may be requested.

1. General Policy

Donations once made are generally non-refundable as they are utilized immediately for charitable programs.

2. Valid Grounds for Refund

  • Technical Error: Double debit due to a glitch.
  • Unauthorized Transaction: Transaction made without consent.
  • Human Error: Significantly incorrect amount entered.

3. Request Process

Contact us within 7 days at support@kindfund.org with your Transaction ID and proof of deduction.

4. Tax Receipt Implications

Important Note:

If a refund is issued, the 80G tax certificate for that donation will be cancelled.
