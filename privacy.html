Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

At KindFund Foundation ("we", "us", "our"), we value the trust you place in us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a donation. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

To process donations and provide tax receipts, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, Phone Number.
  • Tax Information: Permanent Account Number (PAN) is mandatory for Indian donors claiming 80G tax exemption.
  • Payment Information: We do not store your Credit/Debit card or Net Banking details. All transactions are processed through secure third-party payment gateways (Cashfree/Razorpay).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for:

  • Processing your donation and sending an automated receipt.
  • Sending your 80G Tax Exemption Certificate.
  • Updating you on the impact of your donation (e.g., photos of food distribution).
  • Internal record keeping as required by Indian Law.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Our website uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption. Your personal information is stored in secure databases accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Sharing of Information

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Government Authorities: If required by law (e.g., Income Tax Department of India).
  • Service Providers: Third-party partners who help us operate (e.g., email services, payment gateways), subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance your experience (e.g., remembering your language preference). You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
