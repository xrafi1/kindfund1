Who We Are

Driven by Empathy,
Fueled by Action.

KindFund began with a simple mission: to ensure no child sleeps hungry. What started as a small community kitchen in Barpeta Road has grown into a movement touching thousands of lives.

We are not just a charity; we are a community of changemakers. We believe in radical transparency—every rupee you donate is accounted for and directed towards tangible change.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I receive a donation receipt?
Yes, you will receive an instant digital receipt via email for every donation made. We maintain complete transparency with our donors.
Can I donate via UPI?
Absolutely. We support all major UPI apps (GooglePay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM) as well as Net Banking, Debit Cards, and Credit Cards through our secure payment gateway.
How do I know my money is being used correctly?
We publish monthly financial reports on our website. Furthermore, you can subscribe to our newsletter to receive photo and video updates of the specific project you supported.
